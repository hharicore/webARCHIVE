I reside and operate on unceded lands belonging the Woi Wurrung and Boon Wurrung people of the Kulin Nation, I also grew up on unceded land belonging to the Jagera and Turrbul people. They are the owners of these lands, and have been so for over 40, 000 years. I pay my respects to Elders past and present, and extend this respect to any First Nations visitors to this website. Sovereignty has never been ceded.
